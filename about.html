Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort

Here at Pacific Trails Resort, we are dedicated to providing our guests with an unforgettable experience. Our priority is to ensure that you have an affordable, comfortable, and worry-free stay from the moment you arrive until you depart.

Our exceptional staff is the heart of our resort. Whether you need recommendations for local attractions or assistance with any aspect of your stay, our team is here to exceed your expectations.

Luxurious Yurts

At Pacific Trails Resort, we take pride in offering luxurious yurts that provide the perfect blend of comfort and nature. Each yurt is thoughtfully designed to ensure you feel right at home while surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of the California North Coast.

Experience the joy of waking up to panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and relish the feeling of being embraced by nature's tranquility. Our yurts are the ideal haven for relaxation and rejuvenation, allowing you to escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Exciting Activities

Discover the magic of the California North Coast with the exciting activities we offer at Pacific Trails Resort. Immerse yourself in nature and soak in the sun as you explore the stunning surroundings.

Whether you're an adventure seeker or prefer a leisurely experience, we have something for everyone. From hiking along scenic trails to relaxing by the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool, you'll find activities to create cherished memories during your stay.

All-Inclusive Packages

Enhance your stay at Pacific Trails Resort with our fabulous all-inclusive packages. Whether you're planning a romantic date night or seeking a fun-filled getaway, our packages are tailored to meet your desires.

Indulge in fine dining with an ocean view, enjoy complimentary appetizers and beverages at our lodge, and take advantage of guided tours to explore the captivating coast. Our all-inclusive packages ensure you experience the best of what our resort and the California North Coast have to offer.
